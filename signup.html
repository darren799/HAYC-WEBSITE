Join the Holly Acres Yacht Club

Become part of a growing boating community built on connection, adventure, and shared experiences on the Occoquan River.

Holly Acres Yacht Club is in an exciting growth phase, and now is the perfect time to join. Membership offers exclusive benefits, access to events, and opportunities to take on leadership roles as the club continues to expand.

Annual membership dues are $100. Complete the form below to express your interest, and a member of our team will contact you directly with next steps.

Why Join Now?

  • Be part of a growing yacht club from the ground up
  • Access exclusive member benefits and discounts
  • Enjoy social, educational, and on-the-water events
  • Opportunities for leadership and involvement
