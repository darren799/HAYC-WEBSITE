Events & Activities

From relaxed dockside gatherings to on-the-water traditions, Holly Acres Yacht Club events are designed to bring boaters together and celebrate life on the Occoquan River.

Holly Acres Yacht Club hosts and participates in a variety of social, community, and on-the-water events throughout the year. Our events are designed to foster connection, create shared experiences, and make the most of our marina and river setting.

Be Part of the Action

Many Holly Acres Yacht Club events are free or offered at a discounted rate for members, making it easy to stay active and connected throughout the boating season.

Events are designed for boaters of all experience levels and provide opportunities to meet new friends, strengthen connections, and enjoy memorable moments on and off the water.

Membership offers access to exclusive experiences, early event notifications, and the chance to help shape future activities as the club continues to grow.
